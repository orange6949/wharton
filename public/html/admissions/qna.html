Q&A

Q

How you like that?

1. All students seeking to enroll in our online high school programs must be able to demonstrate a working knowledge of the English language.

2. Students enrolling in our high school program must be able to provide proof of completing the eighth grade.

3. Students entering Wharton High School need to show a school transcript, or standardized test scores indicating at or above 8th grade level performance in all subject areas.

4. Students entering Wharton High School must be at least 14 years of age.

Q

Can I enroll if I live outside of the United States?

Yes, we welcome students from different countries. If you are not in the U.S. and interested in enrolling in our school. Please call the counselor at 1-702-9903555, or send an email to us at support@wharton.education or fill out the request form

Q

How do I enroll?

We enroll students in the courses through the Membership system. Students will register for the courses by themselves.

Check our enrollment plans here >>

Here are the steps for class sign-up.

1. Create an account on our website.

2. Send the required materials at support@wharton.education

3. Choose one of the enrollment plans and sign up for the courses. (If you want to enroll in an individual course, please send your request at support@wharton.education)

4. Pay the tuition with PayPal.

5. Access the courses by clicking the menu "My course" under the menu item "Student Registration". (Log in to see this menu)
